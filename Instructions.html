Instructions



  • There are 5 levels in this game and in each level, a new kind of block is introduced.
  • The brown block represents wood: Needs one hit to break.
  • The silver block represents metal: Needs 3 hits to break.
  • The blue block represents a high speed block: Block comes back at 1.5 times the speed.
  • The red block represents an exploding block: Removes all blocks adjacent to it.
  • Some blocks are disguised as wood but are really metal (they will turn to metal when hit).
  • Each level has 20 tries, after which you automatically lose the game.
